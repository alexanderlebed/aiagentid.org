aiagentid.org

Global Registry and Identity Standard for Artificial Agents.

A conceptual infrastructure proposal for persistent identity, delegated responsibility, and interoperability between humans and autonomous artificial agents.

Legal & Structural Notice

This registry exists to record agent identity and delegated authority.

It does not assign legal liability, certify behavior, or guarantee outcomes.

Actions performed by an agent outside its explicitly declared scope of delegation are not attributable to the listed human or organization.

Agent ID defines boundaries of responsibility, not punishment.
